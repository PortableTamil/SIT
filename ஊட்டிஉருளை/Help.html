Quick Help

This help page is generated automatically and takes your settings into account.

You may access these options through the system tray menu or by using the specified hotkey.
All hotkeys are active only when the ticker is visible.
Pin/UnPin the Ticker This is indicated by the circle icon.
When the ticker is pinned, it will always stay visible unless you move your mouse close to it.
When the ticker is unpinned, it will automatically hide whenever you use your computer.
When unpinned. it will show itself after 15 seconds of inactivity on your PC.
Hotkey: ScrollLock
Pause/Unpause the Ticker This is indicated by the Pause icon.
When the ticker is paused, news items will not scroll automatically.
When the ticker is unpaused, news items will scroll automatically, showing each item for 6 seconds.
Hotkey: Pause
Visit Item Visit the page of the currently displayed item.
Hotkey: PrintScreen
Manage Feeds Manage your feeds.
Hotkey: F12
Do Not Disturb Temporarily disable the ticker for 15 minutes.
Hotkey: F11
Options Open the options dialog.
Hotkey: F8
Exit Exit FeedRoller.
Hotkey: F4
 
These additional hotkeys are available only when the ticker is paused.
Scroll Up/Down Scroll to the previous/next item.
Hotkey: Ctrl+Up / Down Arrows
Mouse: Ctrl+Mouse Wheel
Visit Item Visit the currently displayed item.
Hotkey: Ctrl+Enter
Mouse: Ctrl+Mouse Click
 
Subscribe with FeedRoller directly from Firefox
  1. Open firefox, with any RSS feed (example)
  2. Select Choose Application... from the drop down list
  3. Browse to the FeedRoller.exe file in your installation folder
  4. Press the Subscribe Now button
